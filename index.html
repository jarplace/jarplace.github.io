Features

We offer plenty of awesome functions to our lovely users:

Passwordless login

You don't need to remember yet another password, you don't need to worry about leaks: we use passwordless authentication to make you experience as easy and as safe as possible.

User-friendly CLI

Easy-to-use CLI will make your experience more pleasant then ever. Don't get distracted from geeky command line to fancy web-interfaces: we are geeks as well, we understand you.

Human-readable URLs

No more unreadable hashes in repositories' URLs: short and simple URLs in the format of: https://repo.jar.place/reponame/
Yes, that simple and beautiful.

Reliable security

We store Jars and metadata encrypted, so no one can steal you products.

Read/Write access policies

Control who gets read, write or both permissions to access your repositories.

Flexible billing

Upgrade or downgrade you plan whenever you want!
